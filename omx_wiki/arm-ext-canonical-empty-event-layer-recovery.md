# ARM EXT staging-window R9 divergence and wrapper invariant recovery

Two geyaxz stalls (sound-prompt input dead at boot; total freeze after the fail-menu choice in play) share one verified root cause: a host R9-sync divergence in the private loader's staging window. When cfunction.ext's loader migrates a trusted extension (graphics.ext) into the 0x40000000 arena, its record/P are reused and, between mr_cacheSync and registration confirmation (child entry fills extChunk[8]), hook_restore_r9 switches R9 to the wrapper rw on wrapper-code entry but cannot restore the child's rw when control returns to the still-unregistered arena code. The child's init then runs with the wrapper's R9 and writes its own init data at fixed offsets into the wrapper RW: RW+0x1C (event-layer selected := 1 — boot bug) and RW+0x2C/0x30 (deferred-command queue head/tail := registration handle / mr_table slot pointer — play bug; the first enqueue also writes a node pointer into the EXT_TABLE slot, and the drain loop at +0xD28 then cycles forever over garbage in low memory, wedging the main thread inside uc_emu with no table calls). The child helper veneer's semantic is r9=[P][0] with P stored at file_base+4; a full host fix (restore-by-[P][0], or displacement accounting) was implemented and verified to eliminate the corruption — but it wakes the SKY promo/billing framework, which then really performs its boot business (POST rop.skymobiapp.com/payOneAsTlv, termsync/gbreg registry writes) and the game waits for a completion signal vmrp does not emulate yet; every currently passing fixture depends on that framework being crippled, so the full fix is deferred until that chain is emulated. Until then the host repairs the two damaged wrapper invariants on structural evidence at wrapper arm_ext_call entry: arm_ext_restore_drained_event_layer collapses consecutive canonical-empty selected levels only when a lower chain provably contains the primary extChunk (code=1, modal depth 0), and arm_ext_restore_wrapper_command_queue resets the queue pair to canonical empty {0,&head} only when head and tail are both implausible as heap pointers — a state unreachable by legitimate queue operation. FULL mr_event keeps Lua dealevent single ownership of ordinary input, matching the original platform source (initial commit d5ba4af: mr_event_function → dealevent, never a direct raw event to the ext). Diagnostics kept: VMRP_ARM_EXT_WATCH_PC also arms the wrapper body at EXT_CODE_ADDR and prints r9. Validation: geyaxz boot 5.43s and play 92.49s pass; full E2E 27 files/49 tests pass with retry=0 under SDL dummy; gdb-wrapper SIGINT sampling (yama blocks attach, WSL swallows cores) was the key technique for locating the pure-guest livelock.
